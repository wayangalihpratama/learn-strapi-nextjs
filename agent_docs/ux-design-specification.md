# UX Design Specification: Batuan Digital Hub

## 🎨 Design Vision: "The Ancient Canvas"
The design should feel like an ancient manuscript or a classic Batuan painting: **intricate, deep, and premium**. We will use a "Mystical Dark" theme to make colors pop and honor the somber tones of traditional Batuan art.

## 🌈 Color Palette
- **Obsidian (#1A1A1A)**: Primary background, mirroring the dark ink washes of early paintings.
- **Gold Leaf (#D4AF37)**: Primary accent for headers, borders, and CTAs (symbols of Balinese divinity).
- **Aged Parchment (#F5F5DC)**: For text and light sections, providing a soft contrast.
- **Temple Stone (#4A4A4A)**: Secondary neutral for cards and dividers.

## 🖋️ Typography
- **Serif (Playfair Display / Lora)**: For headings, evoking a sense of history and craftsmanship.
- **Sans-Serif (Inter / Roboto)**: For body text, ensuring readability across all devices.

## 🧱 Interaction Patterns
1.  **Parallax Scrolls**: Subtle movement on landmark images (Pura Puseh) to create depth.
2.  **Image Magnifier**: For Batuan paintings, allowing collectors to see the "meticulous detail" without leaving the page.
3.  **Floating Navigation**: Minimalist and semi-transparent, staying out of the way of the content.

## 📱 User Journey Map (The Collector)
1.  **Landing**: Welcomed by a cinematic hero of Batuan Village.
2.  **Discovery**: Explores the Artisan Gallery, filtering by "Classical" or "Post-Modern".
3.  **Detail**: Clicks a painting, views the intricate brushwork via magnifier, reads the artist's story.
4.  **Conversion**: Clicks "Inquire via WhatsApp" to start a direct negotiation.
