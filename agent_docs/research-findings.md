# Research Findings: Batuan Village (The Village of Arts)

## 🏆 Batuan Specific Benchmarks

### 1. Pura Puseh Batuan (Ancient Temple)
- **Founding**: 1022 AD (Nearly 1,000 years old).
- **Architecture**: Intricate stone carvings, palm fiber (ijuk) roofs.
- **Data Point**: Open 09:00 - 18:00. Requires sarongs for visitors.

### 2. Batuan Style Painting
- **Style**: Dense, intricate, meticulous detail. Often religious or mythological.
- **Evolution**: From traditional black-ink washes to contemporary "post-modern" elements (cars, UFOs).
- **Market**: Huge potential for high-ticket sales to global collectors.

### 3. Gambuh Dance (National Treasure)
- **History**: Believed to be the oldest Balinese dance form (15th century).
- **Status**: Rare, declining, and high cultural value.
- **Data Point**: Performed during Odalan (temple anniversaries) and full moon festivals.

## 🛠️ Refined Data Requirements (Mary's Analysis)

### `Painting` Collection (Specialized Product)
- `artist_generation`: Enumeration (Traditional, Classical, Post-Modern).
- `technique_description`: Rich Text (explaining the "Batuan wash" technique).
- `authentication_info`: Image/File (for authenticity certificates).

### `Performance` Collection (Specialized Article/Event)
- `ceremony_link`: Relation (linking dance to specific temple events).
- `is_sacred`: Boolean (to distinguish between tourist shows and holy rituals).
- `video_snippet`: URL (for YouTube/Vimeo previews of the Gambuh dance).

## 💡 Strategic Takeaway
The site must feel "Ancient yet Modern". We will use a **dark theme** to reflect the somber, intricate tones of early Batuan paintings, mixed with clean, high-performance layouts.
