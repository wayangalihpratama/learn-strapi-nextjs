# Product Brief: Digital Tourism Hub (White-label Template)

## 📋 Overview
**Digital Tourism Hub** is a premium, white-label digital platform template designed to promote individual Tourism Villages (Desa Wisata). It is built as a reusable product that can be quickly deployed for any village by simply changing the content and branding in the CMS.

## 🎯 Target Audience
1.  **Travelers**: Looking for authentic, local experiences outside of big cities.
2.  **Village Community**: Local businesses (homestays, crafts) wanting to reach a wider market.
3.  **Local Government/Admin**: Managing village branding and information.

## 🌟 Value Proposition
- **Authenticity**: Direct connection to village heritage and locals.
- **Convenience**: All-in-one information for travel planning and local shopping.
- **Economic Impact**: Directly supporting the village economy through digital exposure.

## ✨ Core Features (V1)
1.  **Modular Visual Showcase**: A branding-ready hero and gallery system.
2.  **Generic Category Directory**: Flexible lists for landmarks, culture, and nature.
3.  **Local Economy Marketplace**: A catalog for village products (crafts, agriculture, art).
4.  **Community Hub**: A dynamic blog for events, news, and storytelling.

## 📈 Success Metrics
- **Engagement**: Time users spend exploring the village attractions.
- **Lead Generation**: Number of inquiries for bookings or product purchases.
- **Learnability**: Smooth integration between Strapi content and Next.js UI.

## 🧱 Technical Constraints
- **Stack**: Strapi v5, Next.js 14, PostgreSQL.
- **Performance**: Must achieve high Lighthouse scores via Next.js ISR.
- **Scalability**: Designed to easily add new categories (e.g., Homestays, Tours).
